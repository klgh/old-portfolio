About Kaleigh

Kaleigh Scruggs is a 28 year old Atlanta native. She has been working in the technology field since graduating from Georgia Southern University with a BS. She also has a masters in Internet Technology from the University of Georgia. She enjoys kayaking on the Chattahoochee River, Biking, Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, and Hiking with her husband and dog.

Kaleigh has been performing standup in Atlanta since 2016, appearing in various places such as Laughing Skull Lounge, Highwire Comedy, 420 Comedy Hour, Java Monkey, and more. She has also taken the Joel Byars Comedy Class and Online Writing Satire for the Internet with The Second City.
