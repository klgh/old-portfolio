About Kaleigh

Kaleigh Scruggs is a 29 year old Atlanta native. She has been working in the technology field since graduating from Georgia Southern University with a BS in IT. She also has a Masters in Internet Technology from the University of Georgia.
She enjoys stand-up comedy, kayaking on the Chattahoochee River, Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, and Hiking with her husband and dogs.

technology

Kaleigh earned her Masters in Internet Technology from the University of Georgia in 2014. She also has her Bachelors of Information Technology from Georgia Southern.

She is currently working as a software developer using Java, web services, and more.
Kaleigh also freelances, creating websites and social media accounts and posts for small businesses.
Kaleigh is active in Atlanta's Women in Technology organization, which supports women in all STEM careers.
